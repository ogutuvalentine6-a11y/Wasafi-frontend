About Wasafi

A Legacy of
Unmatched Luxury

Since 2005, Wasafi Hotel & Resort has stood as East Africa's most distinguished address — a sanctuary where traditional warmth meets contemporary elegance, where every corridor whispers a story, and every suite is a world of its own.

Our philosophy is simple: to create moments that last a lifetime. From our award-winning restaurant to our tranquil spa, every detail is crafted with devotion for guests who deserve nothing less than extraordinary.
